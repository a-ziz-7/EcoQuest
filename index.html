About EcoQuest

EcoQuest is an immersive and educational game developed for the Rutgers University Hackathon, aiming to raise awareness about climate change and environmental issues. Through interactive questions and quizzes, players are challenged to explore various aspects of climate science, energy conservation, waste reduction, and more. As players progress, they gain valuable insights into the causes and effects of climate change while discovering practical solutions and actions to make a positive impact. By combining education with entertainment, EcoQuest inspires players to become informed advocates for environmental stewardship and champions of a sustainable future, fostering dialogue and driving meaningful change in the fight against climate change.

Missions

Embark on a series of engaging missions within EcoQuest, each designed to tackle different aspects of climate change and environmental conservation. From exploring renewable energy sources to addressing plastic pollution, each mission presents a unique challenge and opportunity for players to make a positive impact on the planet. Dive into interactive scenarios, solve environmental puzzles, and take decisive actions to combat global warming and protect our precious ecosystems. With each mission completed, players contribute to a collective effort to raise awareness, promote sustainability, and pave the way towards a greener, more resilient future for all. Join me on this quest to safeguard our planet and ensure a thriving environment for generations to come.

Game
